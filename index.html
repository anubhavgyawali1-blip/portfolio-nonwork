My name is Anubhav Gyawali. I am a 16-year-old content creator, speedcuber, and storyteller from Kathmandu, Nepal.

I am most prominently known as the Fastest Cuber in Nepal. I have held 5 national records which has allowed me to represent Nepal on the international stage. Don't take my word for it. Click here Search it yourself →

My greatest strength is the ability to learn fast, adapt, and apply what I have learned under pressure. Cubing taught me this most clearly: instead of just practicing more I studied algorithms methodically, identified patterns in my inefficiencies, and restructured my approach until the results changed. That same mental process: break the problem down, find the inefficiency, rebuild. That is how I approach almost everything in my life.

Organizing Nepali Nationals 2025 tested me in ways I never anticipated. Coordinating competitors from five countries, planning a three-day schedule, managing last-minute changes, staying composed when things went wrong. The fact that the event ran successfully is something I carry a lot of quiet pride about.

And under all of it is something more personal: I grew up without a father, in a household where every resource had real weight. That reality has made me both grateful and deeply motivated to make the most of every opportunity placed in front of me.
